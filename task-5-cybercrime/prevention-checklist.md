# Stay Safe Online — Cybercrime Prevention Checklist
### For College Students in India

## General Online Safety
1.  Always verify the sender's email address before clicking any link
2.  Never click on links in unsolicited emails or SMS messages
3.  Check that website URLs begin with "https://" before entering any data
4.  Use strong, unique passwords for each online account
5. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
6.  Log out of accounts when using shared or public computers

## Financial & UPI Safety
7.  Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank password with anyone —
      not even someone claiming to be from your bank
8.  Be cautious of "collect requests" on UPI apps — you never need
      to enter your PIN to receive money
9.  Verify the receiver's UPI ID carefully before every transaction
10.  Avoid using public Wi-Fi for any banking or payment activity

## Reporting
-  **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
-  **Cyber Crime Helpline:** 1930
- Report any suspicious activity immediately — early reporting
  increases the chance of recovering lost funds.
