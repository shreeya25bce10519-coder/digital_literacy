# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

##  Do's
1. Keep your profile professional — use your real name and a neutral bio
2. Think before you post — ask yourself if you'd be comfortable with a future employer seeing it
3. Use privacy settings to control who sees your content
4. Engage positively — comment and share content that adds value
5. Showcase your skills — share projects, certificates, and achievements

## Don'ts
1. Don't share personal information like your phone number or home address publicly
2. Don't post or engage with offensive, hateful, or discriminatory content
3. Don't share fake news or unverified information
4. Don't use social media to vent about professors, classmates, or institutions
5. Don't ignore suspicious messages or friend requests from unknown accounts
